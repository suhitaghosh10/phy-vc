Abstract

We present Phy-VC, a physics-informed voice conversion framework that leverages Webster's horn equation to model vocal tract acoustics. Phy-VC anonymizes speaker identity — converting each source to both female and male target voices — while faithfully preserving clinically diagnostic markers: disfluencies in stuttering, pause patterns in dementia, and prosodic degradations in elderly speech.
